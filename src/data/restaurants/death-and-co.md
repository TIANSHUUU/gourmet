## en

About ten years ago I was super into cocktails, and Death & Co New York was a bar I really admired — one of the pioneers of the craft cocktail movement, and the book "Death & Co: Modern Classic Cocktails" inspired so many. Melbourne is their first overseas location, so of course I had to try it. The venue feels like a theatre — dimly lit, cozy vibes. We sat downstairs, which was quieter than upstairs. Tried some DC classics and they did not disappoint! The drunken cookies were tasty, and plenty of other items on the food menu looked appealing too.

## zh

大概十年前我超迷鸡尾酒，纽约的 Death & Co 是我很欣赏的一家店——精酿鸡尾酒运动的先驱之一，那本《Death & Co: Modern Classic Cocktails》启发了无数人。墨尔本是他们第一家海外分店，必须打卡。整个空间像个小剧场，灯光昏暗，氛围超 chill。我们坐在楼下，比楼上安静些。点了几款 DC 经典款，完全没让人失望！"Drunken Cookies" 很好吃，菜单上其他几样小食看着也很诱人。
