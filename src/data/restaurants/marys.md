## en

Everything is sooo delicious! Love, love the bold flavours, even inspiring for our home cooking! The suya beef is our fav, and the barramundi & rice, fried plantain are also super impressive. The owner Mary makes our experience very pleasant and chill, she told us that the recipes come from her Nigerian family and it tasted authentic for sure! 100% recommended, would like to come back.

## zh

每一道菜都超级好吃！大胆浓郁的口味让人上头，甚至启发了我们在家下厨的灵感。苏亚烤牛肉是绝对的最爱，巴拉曼迪鱼配米饭和炸大蕉同样惊艳。老板娘 Mary 热情随和，聊起来才知道食谱源自她的尼日利亚家族，正宗到位。强烈推荐，肯定会再来！
