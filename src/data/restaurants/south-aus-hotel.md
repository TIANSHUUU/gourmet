## en

Love the vibe, service and wine offerings! If you like wines, do try those from Good Intentions Wines — a local winery making really interesting stuff. Our fav is the Volcanic Lakes Chardonnay, no question. Although we didn't order food, the menu looked really well thought-out. Nice decor too, with spaces showcasing Mt. Gambier's history and local artists' work. The staff were all very welcoming — had a lovely chat with Majella.

## zh

氛围、服务和酒单都超赞！爱喝酒的朋友一定要试试 Good Intentions Wines，这是当地一家出品很有意思的小酒庄，我们的最爱是火山湖霞多丽，毫无悬念。虽然没有点餐，但菜单看起来很用心。装潢也很有格调，展示着 Mt. Gambier 的历史和本地艺术家的作品。店员们都热情好客，和 Majella 聊得很愉快。
